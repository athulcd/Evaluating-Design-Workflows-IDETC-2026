black_box:
  inputs:
    - id: IN_E1
      description: Human energy (hand or foot force)
      type: energy
    - id: IN_M1
      description: Rivet (3-5 mm diameter)
      type: material
    - id: IN_M2
      description: Brake shoe and lining assembly
      type: material
    - id: IN_S1
      description: Positioning and actuation control (operator intent)
      type: signal
  outputs:
    - id: OUT_M1
      description: Riveted brake assembly (quality joint)
      type: material
    - id: OUT_E1
      description: Dissipated energy (friction and heat)
      type: energy
    - id: OUT_S1
      description: Visual and tactile feedback of joint completion
      type: signal
  description: |
    The tool transforms human input energy and raw materials (rivets, brake components) into a 
    securely riveted brake assembly through a manual mechanical process, providing feedback 
    to the operator while dissipating excess energy.

  - id: F1
    description: Mount and stabilize tool on workshop bench
    inputs: [IN_S1]
    outputs: [OUT_S1]
    requirements: [R12]
  - id: F2
    description: Support and position brake shoe and lining
    inputs: [IN_M2]
    outputs: [OUT_S1]
    requirements: [R2, R3, R4, R5]
  - id: F3
    description: Guide and hold rivet in position
    inputs: [IN_M1]
    outputs: [OUT_S1]
    requirements: [R6, R7]
  - id: F4
    description: Transform human input into riveting force
    inputs: [IN_E1, IN_S1]
    outputs: [OUT_E1]
    requirements: [R8, R17]
  - id: F5
    description: Form rivet head to create joint
    inputs: [IN_M1, IN_M2]
    outputs: [OUT_M1, OUT_S1]
    requirements: [R1, R9]
